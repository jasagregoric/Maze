Hidden Maze

How To Play

  • Use W, A, S, D to move.
  • You can also use Left, Right, Up, Down keys to move.
  • The goal of the game is to escape the maze.
  • If you are lost you can always press F2 to discover the map, but 20 seconds will be added to your time.
